### Engineering velocity is slowing down

Time from ticket to deployment is too long, your engineers are unsatisfied and are
struggling with tooling.

### Systems are difficult to scale and maintain

Your systems are not easily explainable and confusing, there is no clear design
patterns or structure. Engineers must touch numerous files to make changes.

### Deployments are risky or inconsistent

There is no clear automation in place, deployments are inconsistent and risky.
Race conditions affect your deployment which risks production uptime.

### Your system is not properly monitored

You cannot measure how your systems are performing, you have no visibility into their health.
You cannot detect spikes and anomalies in your system, nor troubleshoot them when latency
issues arise.
