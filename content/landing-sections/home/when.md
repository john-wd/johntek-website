### Engineering velocity is slowing down

Time from ticket to deployment is too long; Engineers are losing time to unreliable workflows and tooling.

### Systems are difficult to scale and maintain

System boundaries and ownership are unclear; Engineers must understand or modify too many unrelated components to deliver a change safely.

### Deployments are risky or inconsistent

Automation is incomplete, releases behave inconsistently or deployment race conditions put production uptime at risk.

### Your system is not properly monitored

Teams cannot see how systems are performing, detect meaningful anomalies early or diagnose latency and failures efficiently.
