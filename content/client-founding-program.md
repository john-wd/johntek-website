# Become a founding client

The Client Founding Program gives a limited number of selected companies the opportunity to engage Johntek Consulting at a reduced introductory fee.

Founding clients receive **30% off their first qualifying assessment**, with the same scope, attention and quality expected from a standard Johntek engagement.

In return, I gain the opportunity to refine Johntek's assessment process, document measurable outcomes and build the first client success stories for the consultancy.

## What you receive

As a founding client, your company receives:

- A 30% discount on the professional fee for your first qualifying assessment
- Direct access to the consultant conducting the technical analysis
- Findings supported by technical evidence and stakeholder input
- Clear diagrams, recommendations and prioritized next steps
- A practical implementation roadmap
- A final leadership readout and handoff
- The option to continue with a separate implementation engagement

The program does not provide a reduced or experimental version of the service. The agreed scope and deliverables will be documented in the proposal and Statement of Work.

## Who is eligible?

The program is only available to selected companies whose challenges, technical environment and timing align with Johntek's expertise.

A suitable founding client should:

- Have a clear engineering, architecture, cloud, delivery or observability challenge
- Provide timely access to relevant systems, documentation and stakeholders
- Assign a technical point of contact for the engagement
- Be prepared to discuss the business and engineering impact of the problem
- Provide candid feedback after the assessment
- Allow relevant outcomes to be measured when practical

Participation is evaluated during the initial discovery conversation. Meeting these conditions does not guarantee acceptance because availability is limited.

## How the discount works

The founding-client discount is calculated against Johntek's standard fee for the agreed assessment.

For example:

> Standard assessment fee: $10,000  
> Founding-client discount: $3,000  
> Final professional fee: $7,000

The exact standard fee, discount and final engagement price will be shown separately in the proposal.

## What I ask from founding clients

Founding clients are asked to help Johntek improve and demonstrate its services by:

- Providing honest feedback about the engagement
- Sharing relevant before-and-after metrics when available
- Participating in a short post-engagement review
- Considering an honest testimonial after successful delivery
- Considering permission for an anonymized case study

A positive testimonial is never required. Client names, logos, quotations and confidential information will only be published with separate written approval.

## Program validity

The Client Founding Program is available for qualifying agreements signed through **December 31, 2026**.

Availability is limited and the program may close before that date once the available founding-client engagements have been filled.

A proposal must be accepted and the required initial payment received before the expiration date. Engagement scheduling remains subject to Johntek's availability.

## Program terms

- Participation is available only to companies selected by Johntek Consulting.
- The 30% discount applies to one qualifying assessment per company.
- The discount applies only to the professional service fee stated in the proposal.
- Implementation work, embedded services, change requests, travel, taxes, third-party tools and other expenses are excluded unless the proposal explicitly states otherwise.
- The discount cannot be combined with another promotion, credit or negotiated reduction.
- Any work outside the agreed scope requires a Change Order or separate Statement of Work.
- Standard contract, payment, access, confidentiality and client-responsibility terms continue to apply.
- The program has no cash value and cannot be transferred to another company.
- Johntek may modify or close the program for future applicants, but changes will not affect an agreement that has already been signed.
- If this page conflicts with a signed proposal, Master Services Agreement or Statement of Work, the signed documents take precedence.
