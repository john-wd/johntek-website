## Turn telemetry into operational understanding

Collecting logs and metrics is not the same as knowing whether customers are receiving a reliable service. This
assessment connects telemetry, incident workflows and user journeys so teams can detect meaningful problems earlier and
diagnose them faster.

This assessment provides a path to create dashboards and alerts so your teams can proactively detect issues
(performance, availability, errors) and take corrective action before they impact your customers.

## How the engagement works

We begin with critical services, user journeys and recent operational pain. Existing telemetry, dashboards and incident
evidence are then reviewed to determine what the team can detect, explain and measure today.

We then meet with key stakeholders to discuss current plans of action and practices used during incident responses. A
tabletop exercise may be held to simulate incident response scenarios and gather feedback on the proposed plan.

The resulting plan prioritizes signals tied to customer and business impact, removes noisy or low-value practices and
establishes a realistic path toward stronger operational insight. It will also provide a plan for what telemetry data
should be collected, processed and visualized, and what alerts should be set up.
